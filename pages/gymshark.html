Gymshark

Gymshark is a uk-based fitness clothing and accessories company, which manufactures and own a website. Gymshark was created by a teenager, Ben Francis, in 2012 with a group of friends. The company orginally did screen printing in a garage, until it became widely recognisable and well known. Gymshark has customers and social media followers in 131 different countries. This growth is attributed to Gymshark's devotion to creating effective and innovative performance wear. Their official vision statement is, "Before there is an action, there is an idea. We exist to create the tools that help people unlock their full, incredible potential and put their ideas into action. Be all that you imagined you could be. Be a visionary. We exist to unite the conditioning community."


Clothing itself

Gymshark creates clothing for both Men and Women, along with accessories like resitance bands and bags. Largest sizes I've spotted are XXL for men and XL for women. Prices aren't exactly cheap, with men's shirts starting at 20 dollars, but you get high quality for a good price. With very stylish designs, you'll look good and last longer in the gym.
